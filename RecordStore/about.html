Who is Lil Uzi Vert?

We are passionate about the American rapper, singer, and songwriter, Lil Uzi Vert. Born and raised in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania,
they gained initial recognition following the release of the commercial mixtape Luv Is Rage (2015), which led to a recording contract
with Atlantic Records, to whom they signed under DJ Drama's Generation Now imprint.

Our store offers a wide variety of records, from Lil Uzi Verts classic hits to their latest releases. Come visit us!
